About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Arinjoy (AJ) Das, a junior studying Computer Science at the University of Michigan. Go Blue! From an early age, I've been passionate about blending technology with my interests. My journey in Computer Science, sparked by an interest in artificial intelligence and cybersecurity, led me to the University of Michigan. My work lives at the intersection of tech, storytelling, and culture. Most recently, I accepted an offer to join Wells Fargo as a 2025 Technology Intern, and I previously interned at 740 Project, where I conducted social media audits and created data-driven marketing strategies that increased artist engagement by over 70%.

On campus, I’m currently Co-President of the Michigan Music Business Club, where I’ve led major artist partnerships (including Don Toliver, Cordae, and 24kGoldn), organized a podcast series, and spearheaded a full-scale digital revamp that more than doubled engagement across platforms. I also serve as Director of App Development for my professional tech fraternity, Kappa Theta Pi, where I implemented new features and maintain the KTP Life app. It's a cross-platform tool with over 1,900 downloads that supports communication and recruitment for nearly 400 users.

My technical projects include SongSync, a mood-based playlist generator that uses Spotify’s API and audio features to curate personalized listening experiences; MScribe, a Chrome extension that auto-summarizes UMich lecture transcripts using AI and RAG; and a Drone Delivery Optimizer, which uses graph algorithms to calculate efficient delivery routes. I’ve developed these tools using Python, C++, React, Flutter, Firebase, FastAPI, and more, always with a focus on user experience and accessibility.

Outside of coding, I’m a DJ who performs at venues around Ann Arbor, a podcast host, and a storyteller at heart. My goal is to keep building tech that resonates with people and creates space for creativity, clarity, and connection. Thank you for visiting my portfolio!
